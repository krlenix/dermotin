## APPROVED MARKETING STATEMENTS (Can Use)

### 1. Product Description
✅ **"BIOMELIS KAPI je dodatak ishrani na bazi vodeno-etanolnih ekstrakata ploda badelja, lista koprive, korena maslačka, korena čička, herbe rastavića i herbe lazarkinje."**

### 2. Functional Properties (Officially Approved)
✅ **"Zahvaljujući sadržaju silimarina, badel ispolјava regenerativno delovanje na hepatocite, deluje kao hepatoprotektiv i doprinosi zdravlјu jetre."**
- *Thanks to silymarin content, milk thistle acts regeneratively on hepatocytes, functions as hepatoprotective and contributes to liver health.*

✅ **"Maslačak doprinosi normalnoj funkciji žlezuda i jetre."**
- *Dandelion contributes to normal gallbladder and liver function.*

✅ **"Rastić ima zaštitni efekat na hepatocite."**
- *Burdock root has protective effects on hepatocytes.*

✅ **"Čičak poboljšava varenje i podstiče metabolizam masti i glukoze."**
- *Burdock improves digestion and stimulates fat and glucose metabolism.*

✅ **"Lazarkinja povoljno utiče na proces varenja."**
- *Woodruff positively affects the digestion process.*

### 3. Composition Claims
✅ **"Sastojci: vodeno-etanolni ekstrakti ploda badelja, lista koprive, korena maslačka, korena čička, herbe rastavića i herbe lazarkinje."**

### 4. Energy Information
✅ **"Energetska vrednost dnevnog unosa manja od 50 kJ (12 kcal)."**

### 5. Usage Instructions (Approved)
✅ **"Preporučena upotreba proizvoda: odrasli, 3 puta dnevno po 40 kapi, rastvoreno u malo vode."**

### 6. Safety Claims
✅ **"Proizvod nije namenjen osobama preosetljivim na bilo koji sastojak, trudnicama, dojiljama i osobama mlađim od 18 godina."**

## PROHIBITED MARKETING CLAIMS (Cannot Use)

### ❌ Medical/Therapeutic Claims
- **Cannot claim to "treat," "cure," or "heal" any disease**
- **Cannot claim to "prevent" diseases**
- **Cannot make specific medical claims about liver diseases**
- **Cannot claim pharmaceutical effects**

### ❌ Exaggerated Health Claims
- **Cannot claim "miraculous" or "guaranteed" results**
- **Cannot claim to be "medicine" or "drug"**
- **Cannot make comparative claims with pharmaceuticals**

### ❌ Unsupported Claims
- **Cannot make claims not supported by the official faculty opinion**
- **Cannot add ingredients or effects not mentioned in the official document**

## SUMMARY FOR MARKETING USE

**✅ USE THESE APPROVED CLAIMS:**
- Support for liver health and hepatocyte function
- Normal gallbladder and liver function support  
- Digestion improvement and metabolism support
- Natural herbal composition with specific extracts

**❌ AVOID THESE PROHIBITED CLAIMS:**
- Medical treatment claims
- Disease cure or prevention claims
- Pharmaceutical comparisons
- Exaggerated or unsupported health benefits

**⚠️ ALWAYS INCLUDE:**
- All mandatory safety warnings
- Proper usage instructions
